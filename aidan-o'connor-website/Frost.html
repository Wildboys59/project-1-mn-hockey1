Minnesota Frost

The Minnesota Frost were established in 2023 as part of the PWHL.

he Minnesota PWHL (Professional Women's Hockey League) team is part of a league that aims to elevate women's professional hockey. The league was announced in late 2022, aiming to establish a sustainable and competitive platform for female athletes.Minnesota has a rich hockey tradition, and its inclusion in the PWHL reflects the state's strong support for women’s sports. The team aims to build on the state's legacy, with a focus on community engagement and youth development. s part of the PWHL, the Minnesota team competes against other franchises in the league, which is dedicated to providing high-level competition and promoting women's hockey on a larger scale. The league aims to gain visibility and foster growth in women’s hockey, with the hopes of drawing fans and encouraging participation at all levels. They captured the Walter Cup title in their first season.
